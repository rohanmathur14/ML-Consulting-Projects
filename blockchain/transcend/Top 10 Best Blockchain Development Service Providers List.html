Blockchain Technology- The Overview

Blockchain technology is changing the way people work and sustain themselves. It is re-inventing the way we conduct transactions of money and information.

Simply defined, it is the systematic way of recording information such that it is impossible to hack or change the system. It is an immutable, shared, and decentralized digital ledger that facilitates the process of tracking assets and recording transactions in a business network.

Though blockchain is one of the emerging technologies, and many are gripped with the benefits of technology. It not only enwraps the financial industry but also exerts a pull on other sectors such as health centers, insurance, online shopping portals, aviation, media, government units, etc.
