Video Content For Meter Replacement Company

15 September, 2021

Unipro Techno Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. is one of the renowned construction companies, entrusted with water supply projects.

The new working plan initiated by the company was to exchange the existing household water supply meters in the district of Jaipur with the new ones.

The company used traditional ways to capture the data and manage it.

The employees used to write the required details of the new and the old meter in their booklet which they carried along. Also, they clicked the pictures on their smartphones which was later used as the image proof while submitting the report.

On the other side of the table was an employee who used to transfer all the information manually on the computer in a tabular format. This laborious work took a lot of time, sometimes even weeks.

The entire task was a time-consuming process which in-turn limited their proficiency. So, there was a need for a technology-driven solution that can trim down their workload and increase efficiency.

With the aim to achieve excellence, the company joined hands with “Kodago” to organize their progressions.

With Kodago, they can now manage, optimize, and access their work seamlessly.

The tool helped the company to create an individual File Rack of all the employees based on the areas they were assigned. These fileracks helped the employees of the company to communicate with each other internally, share the concerned data reports, add images of the meters, and create a structured database for future use.

They can now add diverse field titles such as old meter reading, new meter reading, old meter image, new meter image, data of exchange, etc.

Kodago also gives admin access to the entire data at a single time without any chaos and complexity. The moment the new field would be added in the file rack, the admin will get notified to review it.

The last but the most important part of Kogado is its ability to design and produce the reports in just a few clicks. They can now generate various types of reports such as data report, fitting report, image report, and receipt report.

This solution transformed the working system of the company and helped the company boost its performance and constructiveness in a streamlined way.

Get started with Kodago and create accountability, allow structured communication, and foster innovations.
