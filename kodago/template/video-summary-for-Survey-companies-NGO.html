As a survey executive at a survey company, George has to manage large volumes of data collected from various sources and store it securely for future analysis. George used to spend plenty of time analyzing this data and make informed decisions accordingly.

George asked his team members to submit the survey forms of their respective targeted areas.

He then opened up all the paper based survey forms and started capturing the relevant information that could produce the valuable insights for the report. He had to separate the forms based on various aspects such as location, survey title, etc.

This turned out to be a mammoth task as George had to invest a huge amount of time to get the final output.

This tiresome job evoked George to search for a different approach to manage the bundles of data. He was then introduced to “Kodago”, the ultimate data collection tool.
