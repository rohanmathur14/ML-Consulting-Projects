How The Kiraya App Works

Kiraya app is developed with the aim of making the property transactions effortless and uncomplicated. The App is designed in a way to make a smooth connection between the buyers, sellers, tenants, landlords, and investors.

Set Up The Profile

Set up your profile on the app once its downloaded. Add the pictures of your property along with the additional details such as location, area covered, number of bedrooms, etc.
If you are a buyer then add your specifications and expections from the property to get in touch with the relevant match.

Start Exploring

Once your profile is set to go, start exploring on the app. You can even use filters to make your search more quicker, easier, and reliable.

Chat With The Desired Match

Most importantly, get in conversation with your desired match and know more details about the property you are interested in. This virtual meeting is the best way to earn trust from the sellers and landlords.
