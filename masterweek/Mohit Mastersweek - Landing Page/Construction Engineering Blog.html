Construction Engineering- What, How & Where

Successful construction projects require skilled engineers, buildersm tradespeople and their coordinated efforts. They are required to lay bricks, construct frames, install electrical systems and other elementary equipment.

Talking about the big complex projects, there is the need for strategic planning and oversight. This is where construction engineering comes in. They play an essential role in the large-scale construction project by designing and implementing construction plans such as roads, bridges, dams, water supplies, etc.

What Is Construction Engineering

Construction engineers supervise the design strategy and ensure best construction practices at the site. They coordinate with all the aspects of building a project and communicate progress or complications to the project stakeholders.
Simply speaking, a construction engineer is a civil engineer whose task is to oversee the large-scale and complex construction projects that involve both buildings and the infrastructure that supports them.

Different Construction Engineering Projects

There are various projects which a construction engineer is expected to work upon. These include

  • A construction engineer may be responsible to build the strategic design of the large buildings such as airports, office complexes, commercial facilities, etc.
  • A construction engineer is expected to manage the electrical systems of large buildings that they have constructed for safe and reliable power supply.
  • A construction engineer is expected to look upon the completion of mechanical systems of the buildings which include escalators, elevators, cooling & heating system, etc.
  • A construction engineer may also be tasked with the construction of large projects such as tunnels or highways to ensure proper connectivity and accessibility.
  • A construction engineer is expected to create the budget of the entire construction plan and assemble necessary materials & equipment.
Careers In Construction Engineering

Construction engineers have both the options i.e. to either work in office or to work in the site of construction or sometimes it's the combination of both. There are certain career paths a construction engineer can choose

  • Mechanical/electrical contractors
  • General contractors (bridges, roads, buildings, healthcare, data centers, sports facilities)
  • Renewable energies
  • Aviation industry
  • Consulting and design firms
  • Oil and gas industry
  • Owners and developers
  • Historical restoration, and much more.
Job Responsibilities Of Construction Engineer

As a construction engineer, you major responsibilities may include

  • Planning the construction operations
  • Modifying the plans for efficiency, constructability, and safety
  • Estimation of budgets
  • Designing the structures
  • Solving the site problems
  • Selecting the equipments and managing the materials
  • Utilizing softwares for 3D models, etc.
