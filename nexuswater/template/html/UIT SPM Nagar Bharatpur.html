Ambapura Village

Bharatpur is a city in the state of Rajasthan, India. With the total population of 2,500,000 people there was a need to design the water supply scheme of some of the major areas of the city.
Since Nexus waters serves as the consultancy provider for management of water resources, designing of the plan and preparation of the reports has been the utmost important work for the company.
Traced back to the recent past i.e. year 2017, Nexus water joined hands with the district of Bharatpur in Rajasthan and took over the work of designing the water supply scheme of SPM Nagar & Transport Nagar UIT Colony, Bharatpur.
The work was completed with full operational efficiency within stipulated time along with the well-optimized budgeting plan.
The major components of the consultancy work done by the us includes,

Designing of the proposed plan.
  • Design of population and demand
  • Design of capacity of various components like OHSR, CWR
  • Design of duty condition of pump sets
Deploying technical softwares for designing and drawing.
  • Design of economical size of rising main
  • Design of distribution system using software
  • Preparation of distribution pipeline map
  • Campus GA drawings
  • Drawings of thrust block, valve chambers
  • Other drawings as per the scope of work
Budgeting and printing of reports.
  • Estimation of various components based on prevailing BSRs.
  • Submission of detailed project report and DPR
