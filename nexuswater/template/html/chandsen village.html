Chandsen Village

Chandsen is a village located in Malpura Tehsil of Tonk district in Rajasthan, India. Chandsen is the gram panchayat of Chandsen Village. With a total geographical area of approximately 3800 hectares and a total population of around 7000 people, the village comes under the Malpura assembly and Tonk-Sawai Madhopur parliamentary constituency.
Recently in 2021, the village came up with the demand of the preparation of the detailed project report under the rural water supply scheme. The work order for the preparation of the same was issued to Nexus Waters.
The villages covered were Ambapura, Tordi and 7 other main habitations that come under the umbrella of Rural Water Supply Scheme under JJM , District Tonk, Rajasthan.
With the aim of providing the standardized services, Nexus Waters surveyed the prevailing issues in the village and prepared the index plan. The work was completed with full operational efficiency within stipulated time along with the well-optimized budgeting plan.
The major components of the consultancy work done by the us includes,

Preparation of the various plans and reports.
  • Preparation of village index plan
  • Preparation of technical report
Designing of the proposed plan.
  • Forecasting of population and design demand
  • Source design
  • Techno-economic design
  • Selection and design of pump sets, capacities of CWR, OHSR, etc as per the guidelines
Deploying technical softwares for designing and drawing.
  • Complete hydraulic design of distribution network in Water gem software.
  • Preparation of Auto CAD drawing with hydraulic design output details such as pipe no, node no, pipe length, pressure at nod, etc.
  • GA Drawings of valve chambers, ESR, GLSR, CWR, etc.
  • Preparation of conceptual drawings
Budgeting and printing of reports.
  • Estimation of various components based on prevailing BSRs.
  • Printing of DPR, and much more.
