Malpura Village

Malpura is the tehsil in the Tonk district of Rajasthan, India. The total geographical area of the town is around 1490 km^2 with a total population of around 2,50,000 people. Traced back in 2020, the town issued a work order to prepare the project report for urban water supply scheme.
To achieve the satisfactory results within the stipulated time, the team at Nexus waters worked enthusiastically in an organized way to prepare the detailed project report for the Urban Water Supply Scheme of Malpura town, District Tonk, Rajasthan.
The work was completed with full operational efficiency within stipulated time along with the well-optimized budgeting plan.
The major components of the consultancy work done by the us includes,

Designing of the proposed plan.
  • Formation of the water distribution zones of the entire town
  • Design of zonal population and zonal water demand of entire town
  • Design of the capacity of reservoirs
  • Preparation of map of distribution zone
Deploying technical softwares for designing and drawing.
  • Taking levels from satellite and plotting on map
  • Design of distribution system using computer software
  • Design of economical size of rising mains
  • Design of electro-mechanical installations in pump house
  • Preparing drawings on distribution zone
Budgeting and printing of reports.
  • Detailed estimates on BSR rates
  • Detailed costing of the project
  • Preparation of tender document
