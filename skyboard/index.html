Education As An Asset

We are going to face an education crisis of massive proportions in rural and semi-urban India, the likes of which we have not seen before. Digitalisation of education through ingenious and well-planned interventions is the only way forward, and if we do it comprehensively, it can address various problems ailing the education system of the country.

Prioritising education can encourage them to actively participate in the economic and cultural development of their communities. This further leads to sustained rural development, a better quality of life, and improved personal development. With the knowledge and power of education, these communities can flourish.

Presently we are focusing on the 3 key reasons for the increase in dropout rates of the students from regional schools. These are poverty, accessibility, and availability. Skyboard is the solution to all these problems and is the pilot for future endeavours.

An Opportunity For Differently-abled Students

Differently-abled students sometimes do not have the right to access schools due to lack of infrastructure, or sometimes discriminations. Skyboard has opened the door for them to complete their education and become an asset to their families.

Decline In The Drop-Out Rates

The dropout students will grow up to vote or worse, contest elections. These students cannot spend 12 years to get their education completed as they have to share some or little of the financial burden to have a stable living. We can help them to pursue the entire education in less time so that they can get a good job for a better living.

Intent To Educate Girls
