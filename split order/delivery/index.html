About The App

Split Order is the intelligent app that can be used by the serving staff of the restaurants/cafes/coffeeshops to take the orders and generate the bill that displays the individual share of people sitting on a single table.

Distinctive Features

Splitting a bill sometimes leads to calculative errors. The app eliminates the situation where one either underpays or overpays. Split Order generates the bill based on what an you ordered and how much share do you owe. Simply, pay only for what you ordered!
